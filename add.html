MEDICAL



A health campaign is a type of media campaign which attempts to promote public health by making new health interventions available. Many diseases and medical problems have a "health education campaign" or "awareness campaign" associated with them. The goal of our campaign is to make people conscious of the impact of diseases and to provide them information about the disease and also we take care of people who need any medical emergencies.

GO GREEN



Going green is a great way to reduce your environmental footprint and help the planet. Being green is all about reducing how many resources you use, reusing items when you can, and recycling items that can’t be reused. If you’re ready to go green, start by changing your personal habits and giving your home a green makeover. Additionally, change your habits at work and on the go to be as green as possible.


FOOD



Food poverty, or household food insecurity, encompasses both the affordability of food, as well as its availability within local communities.It is vital that household food insecurity is seen as being driven by both the affordability and availability of a healthy diet. Food poverty has multiple negative impacts on individuals’ health and wellbeing. It is important to ensure that people can access a healthy diet in a socially acceptable way.

CLOTHES



Tell your family and friends that you are donating clothes to the poor. Ask them if they have any garments they would like to add to your donation. Collect their items and assure that they are clean and in good condition. Fold all the items and place them in boxes, bins or bags that you are willing to give away.
